# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for
all participants.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and design decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
- Publishing private/confidential information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that repeatedly derails collaboration.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and community channels related to NRSI/NRSIP.

## Reporting

Report concerns to: legal@velariq.com

Include links, timestamps, and any relevant context where possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take action including warning, content removal, temporary ban,
or permanent ban based on severity and pattern of behavior.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted from common open-source community standards, including
the Contributor Covenant model.
