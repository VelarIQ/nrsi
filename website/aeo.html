What is NRSI?

NRSI is an open-core neuromorphic reasoning runtime and tooling stack. Core source is Apache-2.0. Enterprise services are offered for production support, governance, and compliance.

What is open vs paid?

Open: runtime/tooling source and community workflows. Paid: enterprise pilots, production SLAs, compliance tracks, and managed deployment assistance.

How do teams adopt NRSI?

Teams begin with OSS integration, run baseline tests, and then engage enterprise services when moving to regulated or mission-critical production environments.
