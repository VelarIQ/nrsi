# Maintainers

Initial maintainer roster for open-release transition:

- VelarIQ Core Team (acting maintainers)

## Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests.
- Enforce contribution, security, and conduct policies.
- Curate release notes and compatibility documentation.
- Triage issues and prioritize roadmap execution.

## Becoming a maintainer

Potential maintainers are evaluated on:

- sustained high-quality contributions,
- responsible review behavior,
- security and reliability awareness,
- alignment with project governance.

Nominations are proposed by existing maintainers and confirmed through
maintainer consensus.
